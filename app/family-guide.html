The Four Signals

🟡 BUY 50 – 64 Positive lean but not a standout — worth watching
⚫ WATCH 35 – 49 No clear direction — monitoring but no action signal
🔴 AVOID < 35 Weak signals across the board

The Tabs Along the Top

The tab bar shows every run from the current week — Mon 5am, Mon 7am, Tue 5am and so on — with the most recent selected automatically. Past months appear as archive tabs to the right — Apr '26, Mar '26 — each showing the last run of that month. The page refreshes itself every 5 minutes.

Why Two Runs per Day?

The US market closes at 4pm New York time — which is 6am Sydney time in winter (AEST) and 7am in summer (AEDT). The two daily runs are timed around that close:

🌅 5am Early Signal — US market still open, most analyst activity already published. Gives a window to prepare before the close.

📈 7am Final Signal — US market has closed. Full picture for the day. Last signal before the next trading session opens.

One Thing to Be Clear About

This is a research tool, not financial advice. The scores surface what publicly available data says — analyst reports, price history, company filings, Reddit activity. I decide whether to act on any of it. The system has no access to any brokerage account and cannot execute trades. NVIDIA scoring 80/100 on 16 May means the signals are pointing up — it does not mean the stock will go up.
